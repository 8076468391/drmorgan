Cardiac Services

Comprehensive heart care tailored to your health needs

Expert Cardiac Care

As a board-certified cardiology specialist, I provide comprehensive diagnosis, treatment, and management for a wide range of heart conditions. My approach combines evidence-based medicine with personalized attention to ensure the best possible outcomes for every patient.

Areas of Specialization

Comprehensive Cardiac Consultation

Thorough evaluation and personalized treatment plans for all heart conditions.

What to Expect:

  • Detailed medical history review and physical examination
  • Cardiovascular risk assessment
  • Personalized treatment plan development
  • Lifestyle and medication recommendations
  • Follow-up care coordination

Conditions Treated:

  • Coronary artery disease
  • Hypertension (high blood pressure)
  • Heart rhythm disorders (arrhythmias)
  • Heart valve disorders

Preventive Cardiology

Advanced screening and prevention strategies to maintain optimal heart health.

Services Include:

  • Comprehensive cardiovascular risk assessment
  • Advanced lipid profiling
  • Nutrition and exercise counseling
  • Stress management strategies
  • Genetic risk evaluation

Benefits:

  • Early detection of potential heart issues
  • Personalized prevention strategies
  • Reduced risk of heart attack and stroke
  • Improved overall cardiovascular health

Heart Failure Management

Expert care for patients with heart failure to improve quality of life and outcomes.

Treatment Approach:

  • Comprehensive heart failure evaluation
  • Advanced medication management
  • Fluid management and dietary guidance
  • Device therapy coordination (pacemakers, ICDs)
  • Advanced heart failure therapies

Specialized Care For:

  • Systolic heart failure (HFrEF)
  • Diastolic heart failure (HFpEF)
  • Cardiomyopathies
  • Advanced heart failure

Arrhythmia & Electrophysiology

Specialized diagnosis and treatment of heart rhythm disorders.

Services Include:

  • Holter and event monitoring interpretation
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG/EKG) analysis
  • Medical management of arrhythmias
  • Coordination of ablation procedures
  • Pacemaker and ICD management

Conditions Treated:

  • Atrial fibrillation (AFib)
  • Supraventricular tachycardia (SVT)
  • Ventricular arrhythmias
  • Bradycardia (slow heart rhythms)

Cardiac Risk Factor Management

Comprehensive approach to managing conditions that increase heart disease risk.

Management Strategies:

  • Hypertension (high blood pressure) management
  • Cholesterol and lipid disorder treatment
  • Diabetes and pre-diabetes cardiovascular care
  • Metabolic syndrome management
  • Smoking cessation support

Treatment Goals:

  • Optimal blood pressure control
  • Healthy cholesterol levels
  • Reduced cardiovascular risk
  • Improved metabolic health

Women's Heart Health

Specialized cardiac care addressing unique aspects of women's cardiovascular health.

Specialized Services:

  • Cardiovascular risk assessment for women
  • Heart disease prevention during menopause
  • Pregnancy-related cardiovascular care
  • Management of heart conditions in pregnancy
  • Breast cancer and cardiac health coordination

Focus Areas:

  • Menopausal heart health
  • Pregnancy-related hypertension
  • Postpartum cardiomyopathy
  • Cardio-oncology concerns

Diagnostic & Testing Capabilities

Cardiac Imaging

Comprehensive cardiac imaging services including echocardiography, cardiac MRI, and CT angiography for precise diagnosis.

Laboratory Testing

Advanced blood work and biomarker analysis including lipid profiles, cardiac enzymes, and genetic testing.

Stress Testing

Exercise and pharmacological stress testing with advanced imaging modalities to assess cardiac function.

Heart Rhythm Monitoring

Comprehensive rhythm assessment including Holter monitoring, event recorders, and mobile cardiac telemetry.
