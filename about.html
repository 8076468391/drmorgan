About Dr. Morgan

Learn about my background, philosophy, and commitment to patient care

My Medical Journey

Dr. Alexander Morgan is a board-certified cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in advanced cardiac care. After completing medical school at Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine, Dr. Morgan completed his residency in Internal Medicine at Massachusetts General Hospital, followed by a fellowship in Cardiovascular Disease at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital.

Professional Philosophy

My approach to medicine is rooted in the belief that every patient deserves compassionate, personalized care. I take the time to listen to your concerns, explain conditions and treatments in understandable terms, and involve you in every step of your healthcare decisions.
